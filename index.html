Accommodation

Your retreat
in the mountains

Resortarest is nestled in the mountains at over 1,300 meters above sea level, offering a unique setting between tranquility, nature, and breathtaking mountain views.

Each room is designed to bring you closer to nature while providing all modern comforts for a truly restful stay.

Feel Good

Relax. Unwind.
Enjoy.

Spanning 200 m², our sauna area invites you to relax, ease your muscles, and do something good for your respiratory system.

Cozy warmth, soothing silence, and views of nature create moments of true relaxation. Unwind in the Finnish sauna, steam sauna, or infrared cabin. Or enjoy a break in the whirlpool and let your soul drift in the comfortable relaxation room.
